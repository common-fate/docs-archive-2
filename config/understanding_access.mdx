There are 3 main components that are needed in unison to manage access and requests to [Entitlements](/setup/glossary#entitlement):

<CardGroup cols={5}>
  <Card title="Workflows" icon="square-1" href="/config/workflows">
    Manages the length of access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Selectors" icon="square-2" href="/config/selectors">
    Manages what resources to give access to
  </Card>
  <Card title="Availabilities" icon="square-3" href="/config/availabilities">
    Manages what role to assign to the resources
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
